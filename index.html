The New Standard of Luxury:
Rustomjee Ozone (New Phase)

Discover a rare opportunity to own a piece of the future in Mumbai's most vibrant premium micro-market. Strategically positioned along the high-growth SV Road corridor in Mahesh Nagar, Goregaon West, the New Phase at Rustomjee Ozone offers an exquisite blend of sophisticated urban planning and elevated vertical living. This premium G+51 high-rise residential tower is designed for those who seek an elite lifestyle, featuring an architectural redevelopment benchmark under DCR 33(10) and the signature quality of a trusted Rustomjee standard finish.

With all primary modern fitness and recreation amenities situated spectacularly on the topmost 49th level, residents will enjoy panoramic views of the city skyline away from the hustle below. As major transit infrastructures like the Goregaon-Mulund Link Road (GMLR) and the Versova-Dahisar Coastal Road advance, this new phase stands at the heart of an investment goldmine.

About Rustomjee Developers

Founded in 1996, Rustomjee Developers has established itself as one of the premier real estate developers in Mumbai. With a steadfast commitment to delivering high-quality residential, commercial, and retail projects, Rustomjee has transformed the city skyline and enriched the lives of thousands of families. Guided by the philosophy of "Thoughtful Design," the developer ensures that every project maximizes space, natural light, and ventilation while seamlessly integrating modern amenities that cater to the evolving needs of urban dwellers.

At the core of Rustomjee's success is its dedication to customer satisfaction, transparency, and timely delivery. The brand's portfolio encompasses standalone luxury towers, gated communities, and sprawling townships, each reflecting meticulous attention to detail and sustainable practices. With a legacy spanning over two decades, Rustomjee Developers continues to set new benchmarks in the real estate industry, making it a trusted name for discerning homebuyers and investors seeking a blend of comfort, elegance, and long-term value.
