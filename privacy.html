Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy clarifies how user-submitted data is collected, protected, and utilized on the Rustomjee Ozone (New Phase) project website.

1 Data Input from Users

If you fill out an enquiry or call-back request, we collect your name, phone number, email, and any text provided in the message field to provide you with the most accurate project information.

2 How We Use Your Data

We use your shared details to respond to enquiries, coordinate property assistance, provide relevant updates, and schedule optional site visits or discussions regarding Rustomjee Ozone Rainforest.

3 Information Control

Your personal information is treated confidentially and is only shared with verified real-estate advisors or associates necessary to address your enquiry properly.

4 Analytics & Usage Monitoring

We may use basic browser tracking tools and traffic analytics to understand visitor engagement trends. This data is not linked to specific individuals and helps us improve our user experience.

5 Data Retention

We retain enquiry data only for as long as required to provide requested assistance or until the user requests deletion.

6 Withdraw Consent

You may request removal of your personal data or withdraw consent from receiving communications at any time by contacting us through provided channels.

7 Policy Interpretation

This policy applies only to the Rustomjee Ozone Rainforest Kanjur - Bhandup* section of the website and may be updated without prior notice.
